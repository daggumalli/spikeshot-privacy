Privacy Policy

SpikeShot does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data. The app runs entirely on your device.

The app saves your game progress (current level and settings) locally on your device using standard browser storage. This data never leaves your device and is not accessible to us or any third party.

SpikeShot does not integrate with any third-party services.

Children's Privacy

We do not knowingly collect any information from children. The app contains no user accounts, social features, or data collection of any kind.
